Your Identity

You define yourself.Identity is the conception, qualities, beliefs, and expressions that make a person. Who you are is your Self Identity, the way you look at yourself and your relationship to the world. Understanding this, allows you to examine who you are and more importantly create who you want to be. This being said you can choose to be whoever or whatever you want.
